Quick Answers

Common questions about contacting and visiting ILM Academy

How can I schedule a school tour?

You can schedule a tour by calling our admissions office at (123) 456-7891 or by selecting "Admissions Inquiry" in the contact form above. Tours are typically available Monday through Thursday between 9:00 AM and 2:00 PM.

What documents do I need to bring for enrollment?

For enrollment, please bring:

  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization records
  • Previous school records (if applicable)
  • Proof of residence
  • Completed application forms

Who should I contact about my child's academic progress?

Start by contacting your child's teacher directly. For broader academic concerns, you can reach out to our Academic Office at academics@ilmacademy.edu or (123) 456-7892. Parent-teacher conferences are also scheduled twice yearly.

What are the office hours for different departments?

Most offices operate Monday-Thursday 7:30 AM to 4:30 PM and Friday 7:30 AM to 1:00 PM. Specific departments may have slightly different hours:

  • Admissions: 8:00 AM - 3:00 PM daily
  • Finance: 9:00 AM - 3:00 PM Monday-Thursday
  • Academic Office: 7:30 AM - 4:30 PM Monday-Thursday

How can I volunteer at the school?

We welcome parent volunteers! Please contact our Parent-Teacher Organization (PTO) at pto@ilmacademy.edu or speak with your child's teacher about classroom volunteer opportunities. All volunteers must complete a background check.
