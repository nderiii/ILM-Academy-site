Student Clubs & Activities

At ILM, student life is vibrant and well-rounded. We believe learning extends beyond academics, and we actively promote extracurricular engagement.

Academic Clubs

  • Math Olympiad Team: Competitive problem solving and tournaments
  • Science Club: Experiments, research projects, and science fairs
  • Debate Society: Public speaking and critical thinking development
  • Academic Competition Team: Memorization and recitation excellence

Arts & Creativity

  • Art Club: Calligraphy, geometric patterns, and design
  • Drama Club: School plays and Islamic storytelling
  • Photography Club: Digital media and visual storytelling
  • Music Club: Vocal performance and composition

Sports & Recreation

  • Basketball Team: Competitive and recreational leagues
  • Martial Arts Club: Self-defense classes
  • Soccer Club: Traditional sport with modern training
  • Chess Club: Strategic thinking and tournaments

Community Service

  • Youth Volunteers: Local community service projects
  • Environmental Club: Sustainability and green initiatives
  • Charity Committee: Fundraising and humanitarian efforts
  • Elderly Companions: Intergenerational connection program

Technology & Innovation

  • Robotics Team: Competitions and engineering projects
  • Coding Club: App development and programming
  • 3D Printing Lab: Design and prototyping

Leadership & Entrepreneurship

  • Student Council: School governance and initiatives
  • Young Entrepreneurs: Business planning and ventures
  • Public Speaking Club: Confidence and communication skills
