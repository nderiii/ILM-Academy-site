About ILM Academy

ILM Academy is a private school offering U.S. and UK-based curriculum in Nairobi, Kenya. Our mission is to provide a nurturing, inclusive, and academically rigorous environment that fosters excellence, leadership, and spiritual development.

Why Choose ILM Academy

What sets our Religious educational institution apart

Certified Educators

Our teachers are not only certified in their academic subjects but also have strong Islamic knowledge and teaching credentials.

Modern Facilities

State-of-the-art classrooms, science labs, technology resources, and recreational facilities to support learning.

Values, Character, and Faith

Central to our mission is the development of values-based character. Across all grade levels, students engage in character education programs that emphasize integrity, kindness, respect, and responsibility. Ethical leadership and service to others are core expectations of every learner.

Global Perspective

ILM Academy equips students with the tools to navigate and contribute to a complex, interconnected world. With international curricula, multilingual exposure, and cross-cultural enrichment, we prepare learners to think globally, act ethically, and lead courageously—wherever their journeys may take them.

Small Class Sizes

Personalized attention with low student-to-teacher ratios ensuring each child's needs are met.

Proven Results

Our graduates consistently excel academically and are accepted to top universities while maintaining strong moral identities.
